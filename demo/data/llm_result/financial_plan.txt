Financial Plan

1. Write down your financial goals.
2. Assess your current financial situation.
3. Create a budget.
4. Establish an emergency fund.
5. Pay off debt.
6. Save for retirement.
7. Plan for major expenses (e.g., wedding, house).
8. Review and adjust your plan regularly.
9. Set up automatic transfers to meet your financial goals.
10. Monitor your progress and stay motivated.
